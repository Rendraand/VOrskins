Experience unparalleled antioxidant protection with SKINCEUTICALS CE Ferulic. This revolutionary serum combines 15% pure Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid), 1% Vitamin E (Alpha Tocopherol), and 0.5% Ferulic Acid to shield your skin against environmental damage caused by free radicals, UV rays, and pollution.

The powerful blend not only prevents premature aging but also improves the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and dark spots. With its lightweight, fast-absorbing formula, your skin feels smoother, brighter, and visibly rejuvenated. Perfect for all skin types, CE Ferulic is your ultimate ally in achieving radiant, youthful skin.

Begin with a clean, dry face. Use a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil.
In the morning, dispense 4-5 drops of CE Ferulic onto your fingertips. Gently press and smooth the serum over your face, neck, and chest.
